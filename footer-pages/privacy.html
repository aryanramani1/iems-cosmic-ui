Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account or register for our services
  • Update your profile or academic information
  • Use our AI agents for attendance, marks, or placement services
  • Communicate with us via email, chat, or phone
  • Participate in surveys or research studies

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, educational records, attendance data, marks, and placement preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered education services
  • Generate predictive analytics for dropout risk and academic performance
  • Match students with relevant placement opportunities
  • Send WhatsApp notifications for attendance and important alerts
  • Communicate with you about updates, deadlines, and opportunities
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

3. AI and Automated Decision-Making

Our platform uses 6 autonomous AI agents to analyze student data and make recommendations. These include:

  • Attendance Guardian AI: Monitors attendance patterns and sends alerts
  • Dropout Prophet AI: Predicts students at risk of dropping out
  • Placement Navigator AI: Matches students with job opportunities
  • Exam Optimizer AI: Calculates marks needed to achieve target CGPA
  • Skill Analyzer AI: Identifies skill gaps for career readiness
  • Career Pathfinder AI: Suggests personalized career paths

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent or at your direction
  • With educational institutions you are affiliated with
  • With placement partners and recruiters (only with your explicit consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of automated decision-making
  • Withdraw consent at any time
